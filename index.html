Remembering Chris

Let's Celebrate Her Life

The Doris family invites you to remember their mother, Christina Amaral (née Roncary, FKA Christina Doris). Memorial services will be held at Rogers Funeral Home on Thursday, August 27 from 9am-11am. There will be a funeral service led by Deacon Breen with readings from family at 10:30am. Shortly afterwards, friends and family are welcome to join us for light refreshments at Catalyst restaurant from 11:30am-2:30pm.

In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to American Brain Tumor Association or National Brain Tumor Society.
